    Natural Logarithmic (log of e) Function

    y=ln(x)

    a: vertical dilation

    h: horizontal translation

    k: vertical translation

    Tips/Info

    Negative a means the graph will be reflected across y axis

    Negative x means the graph will be reflected across x axis

    Remember! Positive h means translation to the left

    The min/max is at (h, k)
